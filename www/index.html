ENTREPRENEUR WORK

Founder


    A car tragically running over my iPhone led to my discovery of the used iPhone market. I then took the initiative to teach myself the technical skills to repair them.

    I launched my own repair service company, iPhone Repair New England at the UMass Amherst campus. My goal was to develop a business model that would lead to franchise locations across New England's college campuses.

    I became business partners with Dylan Dale, who launched the University of Connecticut branch. After graduation, I chose to part ways and focus on my software career, while my partner took his branch and turned it into the sustainable business, BuyBackHub.com.
